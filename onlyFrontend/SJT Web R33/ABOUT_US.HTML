About Us

SJT Electric Vehicles, Pune, India, is founded by Mr. Jayapal G, Former Executive of Tata Johnson Controls, is revolutionizing the way Electric Car Conversions are done in India.

SJT Electric Vehicles is a trusted name in the electric vehicle industry, dedicated to providing sustainable mobility solutions. With years of expertise in the domain, we are proud to offer top-of-the-line retrofit xEVs and EV conversion services.

Our Mission

Our mission is to accelerate the adoption of electric vehicles in India, one vehicle at a time, contributing to a greener and cleaner environment for future generations.

Innovative Features, Unparalleled Benefits

  1. Affordability: Convert your old petrol, diesel, LPG, or CNG car to a pure electric vehicle at approximately half the cost of a new EV. Retain your old registration number with a brand-new green plate.

  2. Portable Power: Our patented Plug and Play Batteries allow for easy swapping, charging at your convenience. Carry charged battery modules from home to car effortlessly.

  3. Extended Range: Combine fixed and portable batteries to achieve a total range of 250~380km on a single charge. From city commutes to weekend getaways, we've got you covered.

  4. Seamless Performance: ARAI certified and road-tested, our xEV cars deliver optimized top speeds and impeccable performance, even when fully loaded.

  5. Charging Made Easy: Charge your vehicle at your own pace with AC charging, reaching 80% in just 6-8 hours. DC Fast charging option coming soon.

Low Cost, High Impact

At SJT Electric Vehicles, we believe in making electric vehicle adoption accessible to all. That's why we offer a groundbreaking solution: convert your existing car to a pure electric vehicle at a fraction of the cost of a new one. Say goodbye to the traditional petrol, diesel, or CNG engines, and hello to a pollution-free ride that's budget-friendly.

Your Car, Your Way

We understand the importance of convenience. That's why our retrofit electric vehicles come with plug-and-play portable batteries. Charge up at home, the office, or public stations – it's as easy as that. With no extra costs, you'll be automating your ride while saving up to Rs. 2 lakh on fuel expenses every year.

Revolutionizing Convenience

Have concerns about charging? We've got you covered. SJT Electric Vehicles introduces a unique solution: the power to swap your batteries. Never worry about long charging waits or limited range. Our patented "Plug and Play Batteries" can be exchanged in under 3 minutes, giving you the freedom to drive without limitations.

Join the Revolution

Say hello to a cleaner, greener, and more cost-effective driving experience. SJT Electric Vehicles is here to redefine your journey – from revolutionizing your existing vehicle to offering innovative electric car conversions. Make the switch today and drive towards a brighter, sustainable future.
